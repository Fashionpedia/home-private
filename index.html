    The Mission of Fashionpedia

    Fashionpedia consists of a fashion image dataset annotated with fine-grained attributes and exhaustive segmentations, a knowledge graph, a challenge (as the part of iMaterialist) for FGVC (Fine-Grained Visual Categorization) at CVPR conference.

    Fashionpedia specifically addresses the following critical components that involve taxonomy: 1) the Apparel Knowledge Graph and 2) an Ontology of Fashion database built upon the backbone of the Fashionpedia knowledge graph structure, with the aim of training and benchmarking the next generation of computer models for the comprehensive understanding of fashion.
